Help Center

Welcome to the NAMMA SANDHAI Help Center! We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter while shopping with us. Below, you'll find answers to some frequently asked questions, along with guidance on how to contact our support team for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

Creating an account with NAMMA SANDHAI is simple and quick. Just follow these steps:

  • Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of our homepage.
  • Fill in your personal details, including your name, email address, and a secure password.
  • Click "Create Account" to complete the process.

2. How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy:

  • Browse through our categories or use the search bar to find the products you want.
  • Click on the product to view its details and select the desired quantity.
  • Click "Add to Cart."
  • Once you have all your items in the cart, click on the cart icon and select "Checkout."
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to enter your shipping and payment details.
  • Review your order and click "Place Order" to complete your purchase.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods to make your shopping experience convenient:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Net Banking
  • UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
  • Wallets (Paytm, Google Pay, etc.)
  • Cash on Delivery (subject to availability)

4. How can I track my order?

To track your order:

  • Log in to your NAMMA SANDHAI account.
  • Go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard.
